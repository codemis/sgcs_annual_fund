We invite you to invest in the students of SGCS!

San Gabriel Christian School offers an unparalleled education for students from K to 8th grade, and in an environment where the Scriptures are central and Jesus Christ is honored. Our Annual Fund allows us to continue growing the excellence at SGCS, while keeping the school affordable for families in our community. Tuition alone does not cover the cost of all we provide, but the Annual Fund helps fill this gap.

Our Annual Fund goal for 2012–2013 is $125,000 with 100% family participation. We also encourage those in the community who believe in the mission of SGCS to generously participate as well.

Your tax-deductible gift will play such an important role in the future of SGCS. The links on this page make it possible to give a one-time gift to the Annual Fund, or to make a monthly contribution. Thank you for your generous support.
